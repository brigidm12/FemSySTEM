OUR MISSION

Our mission is to involve more females, especially female high school students, in STEM subjects by sharing different computer science programs that cater to high school students. Our hope is that more young women will gravitate towards these programs, as they are firstly free and highly accessible for many students. We focus on providing numerous opportunities for students to ensure that the STEM field can slowly but surely become more diverse. There are numerous women out there who have the capabilites to enter the STEM field. However, many stray away due to its male-dominated nature. In order to increase the diversity of these kinds of careers, it is important for students to get more exposure to CS early on so that they truly begin to understand their own capabilities.

WHAT IS THE ISSUE?

Based on data collected from the 2018 U.S. Census Bureau, women in STEM make up only 28% of the workforce even though women make up more than half of the college-educated population.


Because of this, women's opinions are often overlooked and deemed as incorrect even when males express the same view points.



There are only 28% of women in the STEM field while there are 72% of men.



Only 12.2% of board members in STEM are women.


16.6% of females have prior experience to board leader positions in contrast to only 9% of men.
